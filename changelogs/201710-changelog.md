Notes About non-AMD64 Ports
---------------------------

Due to lack of device availability, our progress in building packages/updates
for non-AMD64 - this includes the PowerPC 32/64-bit Big Endian, ARMv7,
and AArch64 ports - we are delaying this Wave of updates for
these architectures.

- All `bugfix` updates are made available (thus no security concerns as of yet).

We will continue to work on catching up with these ports. Please take note of
news posts on the [Community Portal](https://aosc.io/).

Special Note on MIPS 32/64-bit Ports
------------------------------------

As of the writing of this changelog, we are in discussion of rebooting the MIPS
port, and whether to drop pre-MIPS32 32-bit port (in this case, our MIPS-II
port targeting legacy MIPS 32-bit devices such as the YeeLoong 8089D).

For your reference, the discussion thread is available
[here](https://lists.aosc.io/sympa/arc/discussions/2017-10/msg00000.html).
